After the defeat of Calamity Ganon by the great hero, the people of Hyrule lived in harmony. 
Millions were happy but the few who were not refused to stand down. The peace was shortlived as a wave
of chaos once again engulfed the land. The yiga clan, grand supporters of Ganon have overtaken Hyrule castle.
It is now up to you to stop them! Legend has it, that underneath the headquarters of the yiga clan lies the secret
to defeating them once and for all, however is protected by trials only a true hero can overcome. 
Are you the true hero?
